# Dashboard

The home page is a fleet health rollup across sites you can access.

## What you are looking at

Typical cards and lists summarize:

- How many **devices (agents)** are reporting vs stale
- **Appliance** inventory and recent poll/telemetry status
- Open **alarms** that need attention
- High-level site presence

Exact tiles evolve with the product version; treat anything red or “stale” as a prompt to open **Devices**, **Appliances**, or **Alarms**.

## How to use it

1. Scan for failed or stale counts.
2. Click through to the matching inventory page.
3. Use **Sites** if one location looks worse than others.

The dashboard does not replace site discovery or Meraki sync—it reflects what collectors and the poller have already stored.
