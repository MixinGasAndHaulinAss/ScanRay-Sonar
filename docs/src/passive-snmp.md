# Discovered SNMP (passive)

**Discovered** lists SNMP devices learned passively (for example traps or overheard SNMP) rather than only from scheduled walks.

## How to use it

1. Open **Discovered**.
2. Filter by site.
3. Review newly seen devices and change history when shown.
4. Promote or enroll interesting gear into normal **Appliances** monitoring as your process requires.

Passive discovery depends on collectors with passive SNMP enabled and correct site settings. If the list is empty, confirm a collector is online and passive SNMP is turned on for that site.
