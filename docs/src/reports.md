# Reports

**Reports** generate downloadable artifacts from templates over a time range and site/device scope.

## Generate a report

Requires **siteadmin** to create.

1. Open **Reports**.
2. Pick a **template**.
3. Choose scope (site, devices) and time range.
4. Generate and wait for completion.
5. Download from the reports list.

## Templates

Templates define which sections and metrics are included. If a template is missing, ask a superadmin/operator to ensure report templates are seeded for your deployment.

## Who can view

Signed-in users can list and download reports they are allowed to see; creation is restricted to siteadmins+.
