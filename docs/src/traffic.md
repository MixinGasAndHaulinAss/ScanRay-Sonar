# Traffic

**Traffic** surfaces flow-style views Sonar has available for the sites you can access (for example recent flows when collectors or appliances provide them).

## How to use it

1. Open **Traffic**.
2. Filter by site or time range when controls are present.
3. Use the list to spot heavy talkers or unexpected peers, then drill into the related appliance or device.

If the page is empty, flows may not be enabled for that site yet, or no collector is reporting flow data. Confirm collector health and appliance monitoring first.
